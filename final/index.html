    A Narrative Sequence

    Based a selection of texts provided, a sequence of pages, comprising a short story or an essay, were designed using type and images formatted as a contemporary magazine. The project includes use of page grids, to build a series of 2–page spreads, using design methods to promote readers’ understanding and appreciation.

    Posters & Postage Stamps

    Series

    The objective of this assignment is to convey a message that raises awareness and motivates change, concern– ing sustainability, using topics of water, trees or endangered species. A set of closely-related variant postage stamps and a poster, which includes a persuasive fact, were designed.

    Farmers Market

    Advocacy Poster Series

    The assignment is to design a series of 3 posters to promote the weekly Farmers’ Market through the context of encouraging ‘better eating for better health’. The series of 3 posters will be used as an awareness campaign on public transportation systems (in and on subways and buses, bus shelters) and on free-standing street kiosks and possibly banners in the Mission Hill neighborhood and the greater Boston area.
